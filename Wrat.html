About WRATCOIN

WRATCOIN (WRTC) is a decentralized fundraising token designed to finance the development of Pharos while creating an engaged community ecosystem.

Decentralized Funding

WRTC provides a transparent, community-driven funding mechanism for Pharos development, removing traditional barriers to project financing.

Community Ecosystem

Active participation is rewarded, creating a vibrant ecosystem where token holders directly influence Pharos development priorities.

Secure & Transparent

Built on blockchain technology, WRTC ensures secure transactions and complete transparency in fund allocation and development progress.
